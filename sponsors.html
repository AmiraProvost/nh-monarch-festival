Support the Festival

Become a Festival Sponsor

Each August, families and the community gather from across New England to celebrate the monarch butterfly. Your sponsorship helps cover festival costs, supports monarch education and conservation, and puts your business in front of an engaged, nature-loving crowd.

Why Sponsor

Good for Your Business. Good for the Monarchs.

The NH Monarch Festival draws families, conservationists, educators, and nature lovers from across the region — a uniquely engaged audience that supports local businesses who share their values.

🦋

Community Visibility

Put your business in front of hundreds of festival attendees — local families, educators, and conservationists who actively support community-minded businesses.

🌱

Conservation Impact

Align your brand with one of New Hampshire's most beloved conservation events. Show your community that your business cares about the environment.

📣

Year-Round Exposure

Higher tiers include logo placement on the festival website, giving you visibility long before and after the August event — not just one day of signage.

Sponsorship Tiers

Choose Your Level

Every tier makes a real difference. Start where it feels right — and grow with us year over year.

Tier 1: Egg

$50
  • Name listed on website Sponsors page
  • Name on printed “Thank You” sign at entrance
  • Shoutout in a grouped social media post

Tier 2: Caterpillar

$100

Everything in Egg, plus:

  • Logo (not just name) on printed sponsor sign
  • Logo (not just name) in the footer of every website page
  • Listed in the printed festival program

Tier 3: Chrysalis

$250

Everything in Caterpillar, plus:

  • Logo featured on homepage Sponsors section
  • Logo on signage at a science station

Tier 4: Butterfly

$500

Everything in Chrysalis, plus:

  • Top billing — largest logo on homepage banner
  • Named sponsor of a science station (e.g. “Migration Station presented by [Your Business]”)
  • Logo on event T-shirts, if produced
  • Dedicated thank-you post with event photos
