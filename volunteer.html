Get Involved

Volunteer with Us

The NH Monarch Festival wouldn't be possible without the generous support of our volunteers. Whether you're passionate about conservation, love working with kids, or just want to be part of something special — we'd love to have you on our team!

Why Volunteer

What's In It for You

Volunteering at the NH Monarch Festival is a rewarding way to give back to your community while spending a beautiful summer day surrounded by nature, monarchs, and like-minded people.

🦋

Connect with Nature

Get up close with monarch butterflies, milkweed, and conservation experts. It's a hands-on experience unlike any other.

🌿

Support Your Community

Help create a memorable experience for hundreds of families, children, and nature lovers across New Hampshire.

🌟

Make a Difference

Every volunteer hour directly supports monarch conservation and helps raise awareness for one of nature's most remarkable migrations.

Open Positions

Volunteer Roles

We have a few specific roles available, as well as opportunities for general helpers. All volunteers are asked to be available for the full festival day.

01
Specific Role

Setup & Breakdown

Arrive early to help set up tents, tables, signage, and activity stations before the festival opens. Stay after 5pm to assist with breakdown and cleanup. This is a physically active role — perfect for those who love to dig in and get things done!

8:00am – 7:00pm Physical Activity Early Arrival Required
02
Specific Role

Booth Assistant

Assist at one of the festival's educational or activity booths. You'll help guide visitors, answer questions, and keep things running smoothly. Some knowledge of monarchs or conservation is helpful but not required — we'll prepare you!

10:00am – 5:00pm Guest Interaction All Ages Welcome
03
Specific Role

Kids' Activities Helper

Help facilitate hands-on activities designed for children, including crafts, games, and educational demos. A love of working with kids and a patient, enthusiastic attitude are a must. This is one of the most fun and rewarding roles at the festival!

10:00am – 5:00pm Works with Children Enthusiastic Spirit
✦
General Helper

General Festival Volunteer

Not sure which role fits you best? No problem! General volunteers help wherever they're needed most on the day of the festival — from directing guests and answering questions to restocking supplies and supporting vendors. If you're enthusiastic, flexible, and ready to pitch in, this is the role for you.

10:00am – 5:00pm Flexible Role All Skills Welcome

Ready to Volunteer?

We’d love to hear from you! Reach out with your name, which role interests you, and a little about yourself. We’ll be in touch with next steps.
