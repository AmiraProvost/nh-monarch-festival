Support the Festival

Make a Donation

The NH Monarch Festival is a labor of love. Your generous donation helps keep the festival going, supports monarch conservation, and ensures families of all backgrounds can participate. Every dollar makes a difference.

Your Impact

Where Your Donation Goes

Every contribution goes directly toward making the NH Monarch Festival a meaningful, accessible, and impactful event for our community.

🎪

Support Festival Costs

Help cover the costs of running the festival — from tents and tables to signage, sound equipment, and event logistics that make the day possible.

🦋

Fund Monarch Conservation

A portion of every donation supports monarch conservation efforts, including habitat restoration, tagging programs, and partnerships with organizations like Monarch Watch.

🎟️

Keep Admission Affordable

Your support helps us keep ticket prices low so that families of all income levels can enjoy the festival and connect with nature together.

🧒

Sponsor a Kids' Activity

Help fund hands-on educational activities for children — from butterfly crafts and lifecycle demonstrations to live specimen viewings and tagging participation.
