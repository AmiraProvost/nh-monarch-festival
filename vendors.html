Become a Vendor

Share your products, crafts, or services with hundreds of nature-lovers, families, and conservation enthusiasts at the 14th Annual NH Monarch Festival. We welcome artisans, small businesses, food vendors, and mission-aligned organizations.

What to Expect

Festival Vendor Details

The NH Monarch Festival draws attendees of all ages from across New Hampshire and beyond. It's a wonderful opportunity to connect with your community while supporting monarch conservation.

📅

Event Date & Hours

Saturday, August 22, 2026 from 10am to 5pm at Belmont Village, New Hampshire (location pending). Setup begins at 8am.

🦋

Who We Welcome

Artisans, crafters, food vendors, nonprofits, conservation organizations, and businesses whose products or mission align with our nature-focused community event.

📬

Application Review

All applications are reviewed by our team. You will be contacted at the email you provide with confirmation or follow-up questions within a few weeks of applying.
