Meet Your Vendors

We're thrilled to welcome an incredible lineup of vendors: conservationists, local artisans, nurseries, community organizations, and more. From fresh food and sweet treats to handmade crafts and conservation groups, here's who you'll see at this year's festival:

Petals in the Pines

A seasonal flower farm in Canterbury offering field-grown, pick-your-own blooms alongside nature trails, labyrinths, and nature-themed programs for kids and families.

petalsinthepines.com →

NH Audubon

A nonprofit dedicated to protecting New Hampshire's birds and wildlife through conservation, education, and 7,500 acres of sanctuary land across the state.

nhaudubon.org →

Tanglewood Hollow

A Concord shop and discovery space offering nature-inspired curriculum, trail gear, and apparel for budding young explorers.

tanglewoodhollow.com →

Avisha Pinecone Keepsakes

Nature-inspired keepsakes and handmade gifts crafted with pinecones and natural materials.

Visit on Facebook →

Mae Florals

Fresh-cut flower arrangements and floral gifts for everyday moments and special occasions.

Visit on Facebook →

Lofty Ambitions Art

Vibrant custom pet and wildlife portraits by NH artist Rayanna Boewe, available as prints and commissions.

loftyambitionsart.com →

Petals & Pages

A small shop blending fresh florals with curated books and paper goods.

Visit on Facebook →

Kathie Fife, Lyme Ecologist & Artist

Fine art photography of New Hampshire landscapes and historic sites, plus nature-inspired fabric art, available as prints, cards, and bookmarks.

kathiefife.com →

Prescott Farm

A nonprofit environmental education center in Laconia offering 160 acres of trails and gardens, plus camps and hands-on nature programs for all ages.

prescottfarm.org →

Wicked Wyrd

Handmade witchy and nature-inspired curiosities and craft goods.

Visit on Instagram →

Buttonwood Shop

A small-batch shop of handmade goods and gifts.

Green Thumb Crafts

greenthumbcrafts.com →

Caliopy Sunflower Studios

Sunflower and nature-themed art and handmade goods.

Fassett Farm Nursery

A native perennial nursery and garden design service in Jaffrey, growing plants that support pollinators, wildlife, and the food web.

fassettfarmnursery.com →

Goldenrod Garden Design

Garden design services for pollinator-friendly, native landscapes.

Visit on Facebook →

Rust & Wonder Metalworks

Handforged metal art and garden decor.

Dangles by Deb

danglesbydeb.com →

HeeBee Geebs Flower Jewelry

heebeegeebs.com →

Heidi's Handmade
